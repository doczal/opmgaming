About Seth

Formerly an ex-pro DotA player known as StoneAxe, it is rumored that Seth eventually retired from the pro DotA scene after losing a grudge match against Crippen, one of the best Pudge players at the time. It was a huge surprise for the E-Sports world when it was discovered later on that Seth had quietly begun playing League of Legends competitively. Ironically, the team that recruited him ended up being owned by the very player that made him quit in the first place. Fortunately for OPM Esports, Seth has put his past aside and is ready to compete at the highest level. Although his playstyle lacks consistency, he has been able to more than make up for it with his superior mechanics. With his former rival Crippen now on the same team, he only has one goal - the world.
