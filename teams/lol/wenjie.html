About Shamopi

A veteran in the e-sports scene, Shamopi is notorious for his unorthodox "casual" style of play. He prefers to treat games as a social chatroom, engaging in thoughtful discussions with both enemies and allies. Do not be fooled, however, as this is Shamopi's strategy of lulling his opponents into a false sense of safety. He is a master of deceit, able to trick both humans and bots (such as those in Mabinogi Duel), allowing him to reach as high as Silver 3 on the League of Legends ladder.
