About Kronokat

Considered by many as a one in a billion gaming prodigy, Kronokat has been playing a multitude of games at the highest level for over a decade. He is better known as Crippen, founder and leader of the now defunct Legion Gaming. Kronokat shook the E-Sports world when he announced his sudden retirement in 2012, citing wrist injuries. The E-Sports world soon welcomed a new wave of pro gamers, including the League of Legends prodigy SKT Faker, regarded by many as the second coming of Kronokat. Unable to suppress his competitive thirst any longer, Kronokat announced that he would be returning to the E-Sports scene with a team of personally scouted pro gamers, now known as OPM Esports.
