About SkullBoy

SkullBoy caught the League of Legends world by storm with his revolutionary jungle Teemo play. Once regarded as a top 5 jungler in LoL, SkullBoy's unstoppable victories eventually came to a complete stop when Riot announced numerous nerfs for Teemo. Many E-Sports critics have labelled his past success as a fluke, and credit his performance to the previous overpowered state of Teemo instead of actual skill. Now, SkullBoy is determined to prove the haters wrong. He has chosen to sign with OPM Esports to compete with the world's best. More importantly, he vows to bring jungle Teemo back into competitive relevance.
