About Fingerling

Formerly a member of Legion, one of South East Asia's most elite gaming clans, Fingerling was criticized for being unworthy of the clan as his skill level at the time was above average at best. To add insult to injury, Fingerling was eventually banned from competing in official events due to his offensive screen name at the time (tam4d3). Determined to change his image, he left Legion, rebranded himself as "Fingerling", and began intense physical and mental training. Fingerling returned to the E-Sports scene several years later, competing in the DOTA 2 solo queue ladder. Now, having achieved an impressive 4.3k MMR, he has decided to sign with OPM Esports and has set his sights onto a bigger stage - The International.
