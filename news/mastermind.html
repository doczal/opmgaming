1 on 1 with the Mastermind Behind OPM Esports

OPM Esports caught up with Rizal 'Kronokat' Zainal last week at the OPM Esports Team House.


OPM: Hi Rizal, thank you for taking the time to do this interview.

Rizal: No problem, man. Just keeping it real.


OPM: First of all, what exactly does 'OPM' stand for?

Rizal: OPM stands for many things. It stands for unity, respect, and skill. It is also the acronym for One Punch Man, a great manga/anime that you all should check out.


OPM: You have been criticized for recruiting unproven players, who are not even in the Platinum or Diamond skill level. What is your response to that?

Rizal: If other teams want to recruit from the saturated pool of high skill level players, then they can go ahead and do that. I'm doing something different. I look at Diamond League players and all I see is a skill ceiling that has been reached. I look at the large pool of Gold, Silver, even Bronze level players, and I see an opportunity. I see a lot of untapped potential and an infinitely high skill ceiling. All they need is some guidance and an elite leader to look up to. I believe I am both of that, and more.


OPM: Alright, that about wraps up our interview. Thanks again for your time.

Rizal: No problemo, homie. Peace.
