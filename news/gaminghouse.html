OPM Team House is Now Open

After numerous delays, the OPM Esports Team House is finally open. The gaming organization will soon be accepting applications from aspiring gamers who want to take their skills to the next level.


"I'm really happy that we managed to secure this venue," said Rizal, founder of OPM Esports. "It's a big house with a lot of drinks and free snacks, as well as a kararoke machine."


For more information on the OPM Esports Team House, please contact Ryan Tong Ying Jie.
